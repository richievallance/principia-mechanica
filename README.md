## Abstract

Doctoral-scale thesis reconstructing the universal mechanical and philosophical architecture of Leonardo da Vinci across the Madrid Codices, Codex Atlanticus, and related manuscripts. Unifies the engineering precision of Madrid Codex I with the geometric and hydraulic philosophy of Madrid Codex II under the governing formula: *Grammatica Naturae, Lex Motus, et Ars Vivens* — the Grammar of Nature, the Law of Motion, and Living Art.

Structural law: Book = chapter; chapter = dilemma; dilemma = law; law = apparatus; apparatus = resolved portrait of nature.
